Model notes: BMR via Katch-McArdle from your body-fat estimate (falls back to Mifflin-St Jeor). TDEE = BMR × step-based activity factor + exercise calories. Includes metabolic adaptation (up to ~10% TDEE reduction as you lose) and lean-mass-aware fat-loss partitioning (protein + lifting protect muscle). Probability comes from 400 Monte Carlo runs with realistic day-to-day adherence noise. Once you have 2+ weeks of logs, the model calibrates itself to your measured energy balance and the badge at the top shows it.
